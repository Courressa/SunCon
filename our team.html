ACCOUNTANT

Has over eighteen (18) years management experience in the Credit Union Movement serving in various capacities including Branch Manager, Operations Manager, Manager, Finance and Accounting and currently Senior Manager, Finance. This experience includes:

  • being a part of the team that successful managed the first Parish Credit Union merger in Jamaica
  • managing the upgrade and transition of the First Regional IT System to Mercury
  • being Project Manager for several critical projects such as the Bank of Jamaica (BOJ) Transition Team and IFRS 9 Transition Committee
  • managing the BOJ Reporting Processing in such a way that resulted in First Regional moving from a state of non-compliance to one of the few Credit Unions that are currently fully compliant with the reporting requirements of BOJ

I currently serve as Elder and Treasurer of the Mount Edgecombe Seventh Day Adventist Church, Board Member of the Northview Christian Preparatory School, Treasure of the York Castle High School Past Student Association, and Chairman of the York Castle High School Parent Teachers Association. I am a past Chairman of the Supervisory Committee of Jamaica of Co-operative Credit Union League, and I also a Justice of the Peace for the Parish of Saint Ann.
